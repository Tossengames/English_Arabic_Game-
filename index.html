How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or touch controls to move
  • Find your lost passport in the city
  • Avoid police officers - they'll chase you if they see you
  • Hide in alleyways to avoid detection
  • Talk to NPCs for clues about the passport location
  • Press SPACE to interact with NPCs or search hiding spots
